Hello!

I’m a user experience designer and content strategist. I believe in a flexible approach to design thinking that's conscious of each project's unique context.

My design philosophy has formed out of my experiences practicing UX in many different ways and places. Using empathy to understand both users and stakeholders, I discover pain points and then craft solutions. I think outside the box and adapt research methods, ideation and design to each specific problem. I plan and design content based on organization and user goals. View my work below to see my philosophy in action.
